Study in Australia for world-class education, diverse courses, and global recognition. Benefit from the Australian
Qualifications Framework (AQF), vibrant campuses, and excellent support services, ensuring skill development and
career opportunities in a welcoming, multicultural environment.

What is the AQF

Australian qualifications, under the AQF, are globally recognized, ensuring quality
education across vocational, undergraduate, and postgraduate levels for enhanced
skills and career opportunities.

The AQF provides an integrated policy that
comprises:

   

The learning outcomes for each AQF level and qualification type

   

The specifications for the application of the AQF in the accreditation and development of qualifications

   

The policy requirements for qualification linkages and student pathways

   

AQF qualifications and qualification pathways

   

The policy requirements for the addition or removal of qualification types in the AQE and the definitions of the terminology used in the policy.
