How we work

Ultima 7 is a seven step process that supports our students
with best possible admission experience

You tell us what you want

This is the first step in our process where we receive the initial
contact from our prospective students or their current
education providers . There are many pathways to an
academic outcome and with this Step we listen to you explain
"the what" and "the when". Weather you would like to study
engineering in a Sydney university next semester or looking for
a summer school a few months down the track , we got you
covered.

We build you a plan

Once we have your requirements registered, its time for us to
form a plan on your behalf. First, we filter through available
universities / language courses that offers your chosen studies
you and match it with your desired student intake date. Then
we cross reference it against your budget, past academic
performance and preferred study location then offers you a
tailored list of options where
you have the highest chance of admission. This is where the
cost and the schedule become clearer.

Together we build and improve
your profile

Academic admissions are heavily based on the quality and the
completeness of the applications made. We guide you through
the process while we together build and improve your student
profile with all the necessary letters and documents.

Ultima consultants make your
application

This is where the magic happens! We will utilize our existing
relationships and agency agreements with top Australian
edu cation institutions and start the application process on your
behalf.

Follow Ups

Based on the specific circumstances such as country of origin,
academic performance and other factors, Australian academic
institutions may ask us to provide further information on your
application. If this happens, we receive these requests and
satisfy them where we can and work with you to seek further
information as required.

Admittance

Congratulations you are now admitted! Australian dream
awaits. Ultima is happy to direct you to a visa consultant if you
chose to work with one or recommend you some
accommodation options for your arrival.

Post Admission Support

We are here to support you with any changes you may need
after your admission. Do you want to change your faculty?
Change State or University? Ultima consultants are here to
support you.

Applying to leading universities worldwide can feel overwhelming, especially since the process
often differs significantly from local systems. However, with the right support and expertise,
navigating university applications becomes a clear and achievable journey.
